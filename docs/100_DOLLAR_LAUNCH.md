# Krava — $100 Launch Plan

> $100 is not enough to train conversion algorithms. It IS enough to find your best hook, get your first 100–200 followers, and build social proof before you scale.

---

## The Golden Rule at $100

**ONE platform. ONE campaign. ONE ad set. $10/day for 10 days.**

If you split $100 across Meta + TikTok, both algorithms starve and you learn nothing. Pick one, win there, then take the learnings to the other platform later.

---

## Platform Pick — TikTok (Recommended)

| | TikTok | Meta (Instagram) |
|---|---|---|
| **CPM Switzerland** | $3–8 | $8–15 |
| **Follower cost** | $0.30–$0.80 | $0.80–$2.50 |
| **Best for** | Reach, discovery, viral potential | Precision targeting, older buyers |
| **$100 gets you** | ~12K impressions, 80–180 followers | ~5K impressions, 40–80 followers |

**Why TikTok wins at $100:**
- Cheaper impressions = more data = you learn which hook works
- Food content performs exceptionally well
- Spark Ads (boosting organic posts) feel native — no expensive production needed
- Swiss food scene is undersaturated vs. saturated Instagram

> If your heart is set on Instagram followers, the Meta plan is below. But with $100, TikTok gives you 2–3x more followers for the same money.

## The 10-Day Protocol

### Days 1–3: Organic Test (FREE)

Post your 10 ads organically on TikTok. Spread them:
- Day 1: Posts 1–3 (morning, noon, evening)
- Day 2: Posts 4–6
- Day 3: Posts 7–10

**Track in a note:**
```
Post # | Hook | Views @24h | Likes | Shares | Watch % | Winner?
-------|------|------------|-------|--------|---------|--------
```

**Pick your top 2 by:**
1. Highest **watch time %** (not just views)
2. Most **saves + shares** (signals intent)
3. Comments that ask "where to buy" (buying intent)

### Days 4–13: Boost the Winners ($10/day)

Run **Spark Ads** on your top 2 posts.

**Campaign setup:**
- **Objective:** Followers (or Video Views if Followers is unavailable in your account)
- **Budget:** $10/day (CHF ~9/day)
- **Ad set:** ONE ad set only
- **Targeting:**
  - Location: Switzerland
  - Age: 22–45
  - Languages: German, English
  - Interests: Food & Drink, Healthy Lifestyle, Cooking, Fitness, Organic Products
- **Creative:** Spark Ads using your top 2 organic posts
- **Bid:** Lowest Cost (let algorithm find cheapest followers)

**Daily check (takes 30 seconds):**
- Cost per follower trending under $0.80? Good, let it run.
- Cost per follower over $1.50 for 2 days? Kill that post, try the #3 organic performer.
- Follower count growing? Good. Zero growth? Check if video is playing in feed (sometimes Spark Ads need re-approval).

### Day 14: Evaluate

| Metric | Good | Bad |
|--------|------|-----|
| Total followers gained | 80+ | < 50 |
| Cost per follower | <$0.80 | >$1.50 |
| Avg watch time on ad | > 30% | < 15% |
| Profile visits | > 300 | < 100 |

**If good:** You have a winning hook. Save it. When you have $300–500, run the same hook as a Website Conversion ad to your landing page.
**If bad:** The hook isn't stopping the scroll. Film 3 new openings (first 1 second) and repeat the protocol.

## Alternative — Meta / Instagram Plan

If you need IG followers specifically (e.g., your audience is 35+ Swiss premium buyers):

### Days 1–3: Organic Test (FREE)
Post all 10 as Reels on Instagram. Same tracking as above.

### Days 4–13: Reels Ads ($10/day)
- **Objective:** Profile Visits (cheaper than Followers, people follow organically)
- **Budget:** $10/day
- **Placement:** Instagram Reels only (cheapest + best format for your content)
- **Targeting:**
  - Location: Living in Switzerland, within 40km of Zürich (or all CH)
  - Age: 25–50
  - Interests: Organic food, Healthy eating, Yoga, Probiotics, Swiss products, Cooking
- **Creative:** Use top 2 Reels as Reels Ads

**Realistic expectation:** $100 = ~5K impressions = 40–80 followers + 200–400 profile visits.

---

## What $100 Will NOT Do

- Drive sales (conversion campaigns need $500+ to learn)
- Build a lookalike audience (need 100+ events minimum)
- Make you go viral (that’s organic luck + algorithm, not money)

**What it WILL do:**
- Get you 80–200 real followers (social proof for future visitors)
- Tell you which of your 10 hooks actually stops the scroll
- Build pixel data so your NEXT $100 is 30–50% more efficient
- Give you content that performed = re-use in emails, stories, website

---

## The "Don't Touch It" Rule

From Day 4 to Day 13, **do not:**
- Change the targeting
- Add new creatives to the same ad set
- Increase or decrease budget
- Panic if Day 1 is expensive (first 48h are always unstable)

The algorithm is learning. Touching it resets the learning. Let it cook.

---

## Pre-Flight Checklist (Before Day 1)

- [ ] TikTok profile is a **Business Account** (Settings → Account → Switch to Business)
- [ ] Bio has a clear CTA: "Swiss-Bulgarian yogurt · Zürich · Pre-order ↓" + link to krava.ch
- [ ] Profile picture is the Krava jar (recognizable in search)
- [ ] Landing page loads on mobile in < 3 seconds (test on 4G)
- [ ] At least 3 organic posts already live (don't look like an empty account)

---

## After the $100 — What's Next

| Followers Gained | Next Move |
|------------------|-----------|
| 150+ | You have a winning hook. Film 5 more variations of that hook. Save $300, run conversion ads to landing page. |
| 80–150 | Hook is decent but not great. Test 3 new hooks (first 1 second only). |
| < 80 | Content-market fit isn't there yet. Post 10 more organic, study what works in your niche. Don't spend more until organic posts get 1K+ views consistently. |
